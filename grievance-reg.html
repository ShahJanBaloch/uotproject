Student Grievance Registration form

The University of Turbat is committed to providing a supportive and transparent environment for students. If you have any concerns or complaints regarding academic, administrative, or campus services, you can submit your grievance through the official system.

Academic Issues

Concerns related to courses, grades, attendance, examinations, or faculty matters.

Administrative Issues

Complaints regarding admissions, registration, documentation, or administrative services.

Campus Facilities

Issues related to hostel, transport, classrooms, labs, library, or campus infrastructure.

Other Complaints

Any other concern or issue that affects your academic experience at the university.

Submit Your Complaint

Please fill out the form below to register your grievance. Your complaint will be reviewed by the concerned department and necessary action will be taken.
