Research & Facilities

Teaching Methodology Lab

A practical learning space where students practice modern teaching strategies, lesson planning, and classroom management techniques.

Educational Research Center

Supports research in curriculum development, educational psychology, and modern learning methodologies.

Seminars & Teacher Training Workshops

Regular seminars, conferences, and teacher training workshops to enhance professional teaching skills.

Smart Classroom & Learning Technology

Equipped with multimedia tools, projectors, and digital learning technologies to support modern teaching practices.

Eligibility

  • Intermediate (HSSC) in Arts, Science, or Commerce with at least 45% marks or equivalent qualifications.
  • Successful clearance of the University Entry Test.
  • Candidates with A-Levels must provide an equivalence certificate from IBCC.
  • Age limit: 24 years at time of admission.
