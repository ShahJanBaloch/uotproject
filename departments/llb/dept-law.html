Research & Facilities

Moot Court & Advocacy Lab

A practical space for mock trials, courtroom practice, legal drafting, and advocacy training.

Legal Research Center

Equipped with case law databases, journals, and research tools to support academic and legal research.

Seminars & Workshops

Regular seminars on constitutional law, human rights, international law, and professional ethics.

Departmental Library

A rich collection of law books, statutes, case reports, and online legal resources for study and research.

Eligibility

  • Intermediate (FA/FSc/ICS/I.Com) with at least 50% marks or equivalent qualifications.
  • Successful clearance of the LAT test.
  • Successful clearance of Law Admission Test (LAT) as per HEC rules.
  • Age limit: No Age Limit at time of admission.
