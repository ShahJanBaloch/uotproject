Bachelor of Management Science (BBA) - 4 Year Program

The BBA department offers a dynamic four-year undergraduate program designed to equip students with core business knowledge, leadership skills, and strategic thinking abilities. The curriculum is structured to provide a comprehensive understanding of management principles, finance, marketing, and organizational behaviour, preparing graduates for successful careers in the corporate world, entrepreneurship, and public sector leadership.
