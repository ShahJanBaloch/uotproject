Specialization (Sem 5-8)

The final four semesters focus on specialized Botany areas like Plant Physiology, Genetics, Biotechnology, and a Final Year Research Project.

BS Zoology Study Plan (8 Semesters)

The Bachelor of Science in Zoology is a 4-year program (8 semesters) exploring animal biology, physiology, and evolutionary research.

Comprehensive curriculum for Zoology including Animal Physiology, Genetics, and Wildlife management.

BS Chemistry Study Plan (8 Semesters)

A 4-year program providing in-depth knowledge of Organic, Inorganic, and Physical Chemistry.

Curriculum includes Analytical Chemistry, Quantum Chemistry, and Industrial Lab work.

BS Biochemistry Study Plan (8 Semesters)

A multidisciplinary 4-year program combining biology and chemistry to understand biological processes at the molecular level.

Focuses on Molecular Biology, Enzymology, and Metabolism.

Associate Degree Programs (Natural Sciences)

A 2-year undergraduate program (4 semesters) focused on foundational science education.

AD Major Options:

  • AD Botany
  • AD Zoology
  • AD Chemistry
  • AD Biochemistry

5th Semester (Bridge) Program

Designed for AD or 2-year BSc graduates to bridge into the 5th semester of the BS program.

Requirement:

2-Year AD / BSc degree with at least 50% marks in relevant subject.
