Scholarships

The University of Turbat provides multiple scholarship opportunities to support deserving and talented students in pursuing higher education.

Undergraduate Scholarships

Financial support is available for undergraduate students belonging to Balochistan and the erstwhile FATA regions to help them continue their higher education at the University of Turbat.

Sister2Sister Exchange Program

A global exchange program that provides female students an opportunity to study abroad and develop leadership skills.

  • Application Deadline: December 15, 2021

ASAAS Scholarship

The ASAAS Scholarship Program provides financial assistance to talented students from low-income families to ensure they can complete their university education successfully.

BEEF Scholarship

The Balochistan Education Endowment Fund (BEEF) Scholarship supports talented students of Balochistan by providing financial aid for undergraduate and graduate studies.

Need-Based Scholarship

Need-Based scholarships are offered to financially disadvantaged students to ensure that financial constraints do not hinder their academic progress at the University of Turbat.
